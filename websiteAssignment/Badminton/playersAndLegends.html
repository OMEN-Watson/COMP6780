Lin Dan

Lin Dan, born on October 14, 1983, in Longyan, Fujian, China, is one of the greatest badminton players in history. Often referred to by his nickname "Super Dan," he is celebrated for his exceptional agility, powerful smashes, and tactical genius. Taufik Hidayat

Taufik Hidayat is a legendary Indonesian badminton player, widely acclaimed for his skills and achievements in the sport. Born on August 10, 1981, in Bandung, Indonesia, he made his mark in the world of badminton with a remarkable backhand smash and strategic play. Hidayat's career highlights include winning the gold medal at the 2004 Athens Olympic Games and the World Championship in 2005. His success extended to numerous other international titles, including six Southeast Asian Games gold medals and four Asian Championships.
